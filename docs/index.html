Show List

Concert alerts by text — track your favorite bands.

Show List is a text-message service that helps you stay on top of upcoming concerts. Add any band to your list, set your US zip code, and we'll text you when they announce a show within driving distance.

About the service

Show List is operated by Thomas Murphy in Brooklyn, NY, and has been running since 2026. The service is actively maintained: subscribers can opt in at any time by texting our toll-free number, and opt out at any time by replying STOP. Show alerts are powered by a daily check of public ticketing data.

How it works

  1. Text the number below with the bands you want to follow and your zip code.
  2. Once a day, the service checks for upcoming shows by those bands.
  3. If there's a match near your zip, you get a text with the show details.
To opt in, text START to +1 (877) 367-2083 along with a band name and your 5-digit zip code, e.g. START Radiohead 11201.

You can also just say hi — the service will explain itself. Reply STOP at any time to unsubscribe, or HELP for instructions.

By texting Show List, you agree to receive SMS messages about concert alerts for the bands you've selected. Message frequency varies — typically 0–3 messages per week, depending on how many bands you follow and whether they're touring. Message and data rates may apply. Reply HELP for help or STOP to cancel at any time.

What we store

Just three things: your phone number, your zip code, and the list of bands you've asked to follow. We don't sell or share that information. See the Privacy Policy for the full picture.
