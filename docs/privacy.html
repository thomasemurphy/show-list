Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Show List collects the minimum information needed to deliver concert alerts by text.

What we collect

  • Your phone number (used to send and receive SMS).
  • Your zip code (used to determine which shows are "near you").
  • The list of bands you've asked to follow.
  • Recent messages you've sent us — kept short-term so the assistant can hold a conversation across replies.

How we use it

We use this information only to operate the service: matching your followed bands against upcoming concerts and texting you when there's a match. We do not sell your data. We do not use it for advertising. We do not share it with anyone outside the third-party services described below that are strictly required to make the service work.

Third parties we rely on

  • Twilio — delivers your incoming and outgoing SMS. Twilio receives your phone number and message contents as a normal part of SMS transit.
  • SeatGeek — provides the concert data we look up. We send band names and zip codes to SeatGeek; we do not send your phone number.
  • Google Cloud (Firestore, Gemini) — stores your records and powers the conversational assistant that parses your messages. Your phone number and conversation history are processed here.

Retention and deletion

Reply STOP at any time to opt out — this removes your record from the service. You can also email us (below) to request deletion.

Security

Data is stored in Google Cloud Firestore with access restricted to the service operator. No system is perfectly secure, but the data we hold is intentionally minimal.

Children

Show List is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect their information.

Changes

If this policy changes, this page will be updated and the "last updated" date will reflect that.
